\section{Tips and Common Mistakes}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Remember to show all your working in examinations
    \item Pay attention to units and ensure your final answer has the correct units
    \item Check your calculations by using alternative methods or estimation
    \item When solving problems involving angles, draw clear diagrams and label all angles
    \item For algebraic solutions, check your work by substituting values back into the original equation
\end{itemize}
